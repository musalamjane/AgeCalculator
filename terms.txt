
The age calculator used to calculate age in years, months and date.

Not only an age but also details calculation such as total months and dates since date of birth.

This app basically developed from ionic framework which is open source.

Excllent user interface which will help you to calculate age whithin very short time.

User has options to enter the date via calendar or manual or both.

Anyone can use this app for finding the difference between two dates such as years, months and dates.
 
Does not collect or share any personal sensative information.

Does not collect perosonal information of the users.
